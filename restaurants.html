Restaurants

Indianapolis, like any major city, has a great amount of amazing food to enjoy. Although the city undoubtedly harbors the obligatory chain restaurants and fast food places, why not choose a new food experience while in town for BPA Nationals? Indiana has everything to offer, including a great many more unlisted delights waiting to be discovered in your short time here in Indianapolis. A map of all our restaurant picks has been included below to create a convenient reference for those looking for an eatery close by, so feel free to explore all the convenient places there are to eat in Indianapolis.

Creation Cafe

With delicious ice-creams, sandwiches, and dinner delights, the Creation Cafe is just the place for a whole chapter victory party. Outdoor seating on the river is available to complete the perfect, warm, May afternoon. This restaurant also has a superbly easy online reservation form, which may be important in scheduling for larger chapters. This form and a full version of their menu can be found on their website, while they are physically located at 337 W 11th St.

India Garden

If you'd like to try something new with some ethnic Indian food, India Garden may be for you. Complete with gluten-free and vegan options, this restaurant is the perfect for those interested in a bit of adventure. Located at 207 N Delaware St, just down the street from the Indiana War Memorial Park, this restaurant could be a fitting end to an afternoon at the park. Visit their website for a complete menu and more information.

Panorama Grill

For a wide variety of ethnic food, be sure to visit the Panorama Grill on your trip, where you can try something Mediterranean, Spanish, Greek, or taste a little of everything. To learn a little bit more about their offerings, don't be afraid to visit them on facebook, or stop in 901 N Pennsylvania, Indiana Illinois.

Barcelona Tapas

True to its name, Barcelona Tapas specializes in a Spanish cuisine called Tapas, offering a large selection of delicious appetizers almost straight from Spain. Just down the street from India Garden, this restaurant is also frequently the host of live music and local bands, making any experience here more than just an average meal. Be sure to visit their website and make reservations, or find them on facebook to see if special deals are available.

Maxine's Chicken and Waffles

An interesting combination, but no less delicious, Maxine's Chicken and Waffles is a perfect choice for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Complete with free wi-fi, this homely eatery is recommended especially for some early morning studying and nutrition before competition. Stop in to eat at their location on 132 N East St, or learn more about their menu and special offers on their website.

Hong Kong

For those in the mood for chinese food, Hong Kong has been conveniently relocated for you to 1524 N Illinois St. Complete with a wide variety of chinese favorites and new delights, eating at Hong Kong can be whatever you make it.

Tortas Guicho Dominguez y el Cubanito

Although it may be a bit of a walk down to 641 Virginia Avenue, hispanic dishes of this quality are certainly worth it, not to mention their equally delicious sandwich and brunch options. This restaurant only takes phone reservations, but be sure to stop by their website anyways in order to stake out special offers and deals.

Elbow Room Pub and Deli

If you find yourself in the mood for more American flavors, be sure to stop by 605 N Pennsylvania to the Elbow Room Pub and Deli. In addition to serving sandwiches, this restaurant also offers a variety of tasty burgers and plenty of televisions, if you are missing your normal sports-watching crowd. Don't forget to check out their website for savings and event listings!

Stardust Terrace Café By Hoaglin To Go

Complete with free wi-fi and a lovely outdoor view of the river, the Stardust Cafe is the perfect place to spend the afternoon working on some homework and satisfying your hunger with a nutritious sandwich. Stop in and enjoy the view at 450 W Ohio St.

Sonny's Pizza

For a more italian menu, don't miss Sonny's Pizza, which also serves a variety of wings, sandwiches, and salads. Widely popular and highly delicious, this pizzeria also offers online ordering with delivery for your convenience. Visit their website to learn more.
